Features

Everything you need to dominate paid advertising

From real-time dashboards to AI-powered analysis — Flores gives performance marketers superpowers across every market they operate in.

Real-Time Dashboard

Monitor every KPI that matters — spend, CPA, ROAS, profit — all in real time. See daily trends, compare performance by country, and identify problems before they drain your budget. Designed for performance marketers who need answers fast.

AI Media Buyer — Dominik

Chat with an AI analyst that actually knows your campaigns. Powered by Claude Sonnet, Dominik analyzes your data, suggests optimizations, and gives you specific actions: "Pause adset X", "Scale campaign Y by 20%". Like having a senior media buyer on call 24/7.

Creative Fatigue Detection

Creatives don't die suddenly — they fade. Flores monitors Facebook frequency metrics, CTR trends, and CPA spikes to detect fatigue before it kills your performance. Get automatic severity alerts (low, medium, high) so you always know when to rotate.

Bulk Campaign Creator

Launch campaigns across 7+ countries in minutes. Define your creative, targeting, and budget — Flores creates the full campaign structure (campaign → adset → ad) via the Meta API. What used to take half a day now takes 10 minutes.

Video Creative Library

Browse your entire Dropbox creative library from within Flores. Preview videos inline, filter by country, product type, or creative ID. No more switching between tools — see which creatives are live and how they perform, all in one view.

Profit Intelligence

Forget Facebook's vanity metrics. Flores pulls real WooCommerce order data, calculates true profit per country including product costs, shipping, VAT, and rejection rates. Know your actual CPA and ROAS — not what Meta wants you to believe.

Creative Reporting

See how each creative ID performs across all markets. Compare spend, purchases, CPA, and CTR for every creative variant. Identify your top performers and your dead weight — broken down by country so you know where each creative works best.

Campaign Optimizer

Drill down from campaign to adset to individual ad level. Country-level campaign management with full visibility into what's working and what isn't. One-click recommendations for pause, scale, or adjust decisions powered by your real data.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Flores?

Flores is an all-in-one Facebook Ads management platform designed for e-commerce brands running campaigns across multiple countries. It combines real-time campaign data, WooCommerce profit tracking, creative management, and AI-powered analysis into a single dashboard — replacing the need to jump between Facebook Ads Manager, spreadsheets, and analytics tools.

How does the AI analyst (Dominik) work?

Dominik is an AI media buyer powered by Claude Sonnet. It has access to your real campaign data — spend, CPA, orders, profit — and can analyze performance, suggest optimizations, and answer questions about your campaigns. It's like having a senior strategist available 24/7 who knows every detail of your account.

Which countries are supported?

Flores supports any country where you run Facebook Ads and have a WooCommerce store. It's currently used across Croatia, Czech Republic, Poland, Greece, Slovakia, Italy, and Hungary — with easy expansion to any new market. Simply add your country configuration and you're ready to go.

Can I connect multiple ad accounts?

Yes — the Pro and Enterprise plans support unlimited ad accounts. You can connect all your Facebook Ad accounts and see aggregated data across your entire operation. The Starter plan includes 1 ad account to get you started.

What happens after the free trial?

After your 30-day free trial of Pro features, you can choose to upgrade to Pro (€49/month) or Enterprise (€199/month). If you don't upgrade, your account automatically moves to the Starter plan — you keep access to your dashboard and data, just with limited features. No credit card required to start.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your Facebook tokens and WooCommerce credentials are encrypted and stored securely. Flores uses HTTPS for all connections, follows OAuth best practices for Meta integration, and never shares your data with third parties. Each organization's data is completely isolated in our multi-tenant architecture.

Do I need technical knowledge to set up Flores?

Not at all. Setting up takes under 5 minutes: sign up, connect your Facebook account with one click, add your WooCommerce store credentials, and you're live. No coding, no API knowledge, no consultants needed.
